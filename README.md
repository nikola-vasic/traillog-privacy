# Trail Log — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026.

Trail Log is a hiking journal app that lets you log hikes and optionally read activity data from Google Health Connect.

## Data We Collect

Trail Log stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Hike records you create: name, date, distance, duration, elevation, steps, calories, and personal notes
- Health Connect data you choose to import — only when you explicitly grant permission

**No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.**

## Health Connect Data

Trail Log can read the following Health Connect data types, only when you grant explicit permission:

- Steps — step count during hikes
- Distance — distance traveled during hikes
- Active calories burned — calories burned during hikes
- Elevation gained — elevation gained during hikes
- Exercise sessions — hike activity details

This data is used solely to populate your hike log entries. It is stored locally on your device and is never shared with anyone.

## How Data Is Used

All data is used exclusively to display your hike history and calculate totals (distance, elevation, steps, calories) shown in the app.

## Data Retention

Your hike data is stored locally on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. No data is sent to or retained on any external server. Health Connect data imported into a hike entry is retained as part of that record until you delete the hike or uninstall the app.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

- **Delete individual hikes**: Open any hike in the app and tap the delete button
- **Delete all data**: Uninstall the app — this permanently removes all locally stored data
- **Revoke Health Connect access**: Open the Health Connect app → App permissions → Trail Log → revoke permissions

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking.

## Security

All data remains on your device. There are no accounts, no logins, and no network connections.
